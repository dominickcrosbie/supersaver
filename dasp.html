Departing Australia Superannuation Payment (DASP)

Claim your Australian superannuation after leaving Australia

What is DASP?

DASP is a payment of your Australian superannuation that you may be eligible to claim after leaving Australia if you worked in Australia on a temporary visa that has now expired or been cancelled.

Eligibility Requirements

  • You have left Australia
  • Your visa has expired or been cancelled
  • You are not an Australian or New Zealand citizen, or permanent resident
  • You had super contributions made on your behalf

How to Claim Your DASP

1

Check Eligibility

Verify you meet the DASP criteria and gather required documents.

2

Complete Application

Fill out the DASP application with your details and super information.

3

Submit & Wait

Submit your application and wait for processing (typically 2-4 weeks).
