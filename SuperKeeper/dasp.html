Understanding DASP

Complete guide to Departing Australia Superannuation Payment

What is DASP?

The Departing Australia Superannuation Payment (DASP) scheme allows eligible temporary visa holders to withdraw their superannuation benefits when they permanently leave Australia. This is a formal process managed by the Australian Taxation Office (ATO).

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for DASP, you must meet ALL of the following criteria:

  • Visa Status: Your visa must have expired or been cancelled
  • Departure: You must have left Australia and not be residing there
  • Citizenship: You must not be an Australian citizen, New Zealand citizen, or Australian permanent resident
  • Previous Eligibility: You must not have previously received a DASP payment
Tax Rates on Super Withdrawals

The tax rate applied to your superannuation withdrawal depends on your visa type:

Visa Type Tax Rate Description
Working Holiday (417/462) 35% Most working holiday makers
Student Visa (500) 35% International students
Skilled Visa (189/190/491) 20% Skilled worker visas
Temporary Work (482) 20% Skilled temporary work visas
Other Visas 35% Default rate for other visa types
Note: Tax rates are subject to change. Always verify with the ATO or a tax professional.
Application Process
Step 1: Gather Your Documents

Collect passport, visa documents, tax file number (TFN), and superannuation account details from all employers.

Step 2: Find Your Super Accounts

Use the ATO's online portal to search for all superannuation accounts associated with your TFN.

Step 3: Contact the Funds

Inform each superannuation fund of your DASP eligibility and request application forms or online access.

Step 4: Complete DASP Application

Fill out the DASP application form with required documentation (passport, visa cancellation letter, etc.).

Step 5: Submit Application

Submit the form and supporting documents to each superannuation fund via their preferred method.

Step 6: Receive Payment

After verification (typically 4-6 weeks), funds are transferred to your nominated bank account minus tax.

Required Documents

You will need to provide the following documents:

  • Valid passport
  • Tax file number (TFN) or application receipt
  • Visa cancellation/expiry notification
  • Bank account details (for transfer)
  • Proof of current address
  • Superannuation fund account details
  • DASP application form (signed)
  • Statutory declaration if needed
