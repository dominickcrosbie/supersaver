Understanding DASP

Departing Australia Superannuation Payment

When you leave Australia permanently as a temporary resident, you can claim your superannuation through the DASP program. Here's what you need to know:

  • Your visa must have expired or been cancelled
  • You must have left Australia
  • You must not be an Australian or New Zealand citizen, or permanent resident of Australia
