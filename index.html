Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about claiming your super

You may be eligible to claim your Australian superannuation if you have left Australia and your visa has expired or been cancelled. You must have previously worked in Australia and had superannuation contributions made on your behalf.

Our fee is a small percentage of the superannuation amount successfully claimed, and we only get paid when you get paid. There are no upfront costs or hidden fees. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific situation.

The processing time typically takes between 2-4 weeks from the date we submit your complete application to the ATO. However, processing times can vary depending on the complexity of your case and the current workload at the ATO.

You'll typically need to provide a copy of your passport, Australian visa grant notice, and proof of your current address. If your name has changed, you may also need to provide supporting documentation. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements for your situation.
